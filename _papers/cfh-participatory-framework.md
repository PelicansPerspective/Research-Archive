## Results & Replication Section

### Assumptions
1. Consciousness exhibits field-like properties
2. Observer effects in quantum mechanics reflect consciousness field interactions
3. Field coherence can be measured and manipulated
4. Participatory observation is fundamental to reality construction

### Predicted Observables
- Measurable consciousness field correlations between distant subjects
- Enhanced quantum measurement effects during high coherence states
- Non-local information transfer through consciousness field interactions
- Observer-dependent reality modifications in controlled experiments

### Falsification Protocol
- **Field Detection Test:** Inability to detect consciousness field effects with precision instruments
- **Coherence Measurement:** No correlation between consciousness states and field measurements
- **Observer Independence:** Quantum measurements showing no observer-dependent variations
- **Non-locality Failure:** No evidence of consciousness-mediated non-local effects
