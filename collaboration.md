- [Open a GitHub Issue](https://github.com/PelicansPerspective/Research-Archive/issues) with label "Academic Collaboration"
- Email: [Contact through GitHub profile](https://github.com/PelicansPerspective)

**For Peer Review:**
- Submit review via [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/PelicansPerspective/Research-Archive/discussions)

**For Partnership Proposals:**
- Create detailed proposal in GitHub Issues
- Include institution, research interests, and proposed collaboration scope

## Academic Standards

### **Collaboration Principles**

**Intellectual Honesty**
- Clear acknowledgment of autodidactic background
- Transparent about preliminary nature of frameworks
- Open to criticism and fundamental revisions

**Academic Rigor**
- Commitment to peer review process
- Adherence to scientific methodology
- Emphasis on empirical validation

**Collaborative Spirit**
- Shared credit for collaborative work
- Open source approach to research materials
- Commitment to advancing scientific understanding

### **Ethical Considerations**

**Research Ethics**
- Human subjects protection for any empirical work
- Responsible conduct of research principles
- Transparency in methodology and results

**Publication Ethics**
- Appropriate attribution and authorship
- Conflict of interest disclosure
- Responsible speculation vs. claims
